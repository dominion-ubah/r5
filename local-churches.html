LOCAL CHURCHES: WHAT MAKES US UNIQUE

Home Our Local Churches
in Missions, Popular Views 71

We are not a denomination, although we have several churches in Nigeria and in other countries of the world. We practice the New Testament principle of the church life which is a basic principle for the establishment of local churches. Our local churches answer different names across the cities and sub-urbs in Nigeria. Our local churches are independent of each other in administration but are related in fellowship. Our local churches are modeled after the New Testament principle of the church life. For this reason we use different names for our churches in different cities and locality.
